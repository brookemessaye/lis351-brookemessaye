About Tipsy Travels

Tipsy travels is a website that looks to target individuals who love to travel and those who also love to enjoy the delicacies of fine alcohol and combines and creates a singular location where they can get information about things to do and places to drink.

For every location on the tipsy travels page, it provides you with a few key items.

  • The drinking age in that location
  • The alcohol of choice by category for that location
  • A few of the best locations to get delicious alcoholic beverages in that location
  • Places and things to do in that location (some that include alcohol and some that are alcohol free)
